# GoDeploy Design System

This style guide defines the visual language and component patterns for GoDeploy products. It ensures consistency across our applications while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic.

## Brand Logo

The GoDeploy logo represents our brand's core values of simplicity, reliability, and developer-friendliness. It consists of two main elements:

### Logo Mark

The logo mark features a minimal, geometric design:

- A black square with slightly rounded corners (2px radius)
- A green triangle (Green 500, #4ADE80) pointing downward, symbolizing deployment
- The downward-pointing triangle intentionally contrasts with Vercel's upward triangle, symbolizing our philosophy of "ejecting out of SSR and framework lock-in"
- The vibrant green color represents "go" or "active" status, reinforcing our focus on smooth, active deployments
- The mark should maintain its proportions at all sizes

### Logo Type

The logotype "GoDeploy" is set in a clean, modern sans-serif font:

- Font: System UI (system-ui, -apple-system, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif)
- Weight: Semibold (600)
- Size: 20px (relative to viewBox)
- Letter Spacing: -0.02em
- Case: Title case with no space between "Go" and "Deploy"

### SVG Details

Our logo is implemented as a responsive SVG with these specifications:

- ViewBox: "0 0 200 50"
- Square dimensions: 20x20px at x:20, y:15
- Triangle path: "M25 22 L35 22 L30 30 Z"
- Optional gradient definition: Linear gradient from Green 700 (#1F9D55) to Green 500 (#4ADE80)

### Color Variations

- **Standard**: Black square with green (#4ADE80) triangle, black text
- **Reversed**: White square with green (#4ADE80) triangle, white text
- **Monochrome**: Black or white only depending on background

### Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the capital "G" in the logotype.

### Minimum Size

- Minimum width for digital: 24px (icon only) or 80px (full logo)
- Minimum width for print: 0.25 inches (icon only) or 1 inch (full logo)

### Improper Usage

- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not change the logo colors outside of approved variations
- Do not rotate or flip the logo
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, etc.) to the logo
- Do not alter the proportional relationship between the mark and type
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility

### Potatosaur Mascot

The Potatosaur is GoDeploy's beloved mascot character - a wonderful freak of nature that embodies our brand's playful approach to development tooling.

#### Design Elements

- **Potato-like body**: A rounded, irregular shape with minimal details
- **Wonderfully ambiguous form**: An intentional design where you can't tell where its body begins and ends
- **Green accents**: Green 500 (`#4ADE80`) spikes/plates that match our brand color
- **Simple black outline**: 3px stroke width maintaining our clean aesthetic

#### Symbolic Meaning

Every tech company seems to need an adorable animal mascot to prove their legitimacy. Some have crabs, others have gophers, a few have actual dinosaurs. We have... whatever this is. Is it a potato? Is it a dinosaur? Yes.

Let's just say we're not taking ourselves too seriously here. But our code? That we take very seriously.

#### Usage Guidelines

- The Potatosaur should maintain its proportions when resized
- May be used in marketing materials, documentation, and error pages
- Can be displayed in full color or as a monochrome silhouette when appropriate
- Should always be portrayed in its friendly, slightly awkward stance
